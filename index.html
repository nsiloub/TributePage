      Quick Overview

      Despite the fact that he wasn't born from musicians parents,
      Jones has become a world-wide musical legend
      by being known as musical and film producer.

      Musical skillset

      He is a multi-instrumentalist, he plays such as :

      • Trumpet
      • French horn
      • drums
      • percussion
      • vocals
      • piano
      • keyboards
      • synthesizer

      Nominations & Awards

      Since 1961, he has received over :

      • 80 Grammy Award nominations,
      • 28 Grammys, and
      • A Grammy Legend Award in 1992.
